Rules for our Slack channel

As with any of our conference spaces (online or physical), participants are expected to follow our code of conduct.

In order to make the experience pleasant for everyone, we also ask that you follow some slack-specific rules:

  1. Do not message people privately without their consent, and do not invite them to join any room without their consent.
  2. Do not mention people who don't belong to particular room in that room.
  3. Do not post advertizing or job postings outside of the dedicated #jobs channel.

If at any time you're feeling uncomfortable due to anyone's behaviour on the Slack channel, please contact the organizers by emailing coc@djangounderthehood.com, approaching them at the conference, or using the emergency phone numbers listed in the Code of Conduct.

When possible, include screenshots or chat logs of any unwanted messages.
